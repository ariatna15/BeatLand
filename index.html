¡Buy your wristband for the FEST!

Get your exclusive BeatLand wristband and experience the festival like never before. With it, you’ll get access to VIP areas, special concerts, and perks only wristband holders enjoy. Don’t miss out on being where all the best moments happen. Grab yours now and secure a unique festival experience!

Rules

  • Children under 16 must be accompanied by a responsible adult.
  • 16-17 years old must have a signed parental permission to access festival facilities.
  • Food and alcoholic beverages prohibited.
  • Each person must wear their wristband at all times.
  • No lasers, weapons, knives, fireworks…
  • Any misconduct may lead to expulsion without reimbursement.
  • Act of violence, discrimination or vandalism will not be tolerated.
  • Pets are not allowed.
  • By entering the festival you agree to be filmed and photographed.
  • You must follow the instruction of the staff.

General information

  • Doors open at 6 o’clock and activities start at 7 o’clock.
  • The festival ends at 3 a.m.
  • The festival has establishments that will offer you any kind of food.

Minor Authorization

Children under 16 years of age

Any minor under 16 years of age who is going to access the premises must be accompanied by a parent or guardian, also carrying a photocopy and original of the parent's or guardian's ID, photocopy and original of the minor's ID, as well as the authorization and acceptance of responsibility form, completed and signed that you can download from this tab. Said documentation and control will be carried out at the specific box office of "Minors”.

Minors between 16 and 18 years of age

Minors are not served alcoholic beverages or tobacco sales at the Festival, but they can access the festival grounds without the company of an adult. To do so, they must carry a photocopy of the parent's or guardian's ID, a photocopy and original of the minor's ID as well as the authorization and acceptance of responsibility form, completed and signed that they can download from this tab.
